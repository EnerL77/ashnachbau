FAQ

Quick answers

Is Evermind therapy?

No. It’s a wellbeing companion inspired by therapy practices, not a medical device or a replacement for treatment.

Do you store my voice?

By default we don’t store audio. You can opt-in to store transcripts to improve your experience.

Where is my data hosted?

In the EU with encryption in transit and at rest. You can request deletion anytime.
